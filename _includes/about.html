About WitchCraftMC

WitchCraftMC is a modern survival server built with a strong focus on RPG elements and long‑term progression.
Our goal is to create a place where players feel at home, supported by a community that actually cares.

The server was started by two lifelong Minecraft players who understand the game from a player’s point of view. That experience shapes everything we do, and we’re committed to keeping the server fair, welcoming, and enjoyable for everyone.

Progression on WitchCraftMC grows naturally as you play. Our skill system rewards exploration, building, combat, and whatever playstyle you choose to follow. Every session brings new improvements to unlock and new paths to discover.
