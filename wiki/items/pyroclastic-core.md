The Pyroclastic Core is a Core that can be used in the crafting of the [Pyroclastic Set](/wiki/pyroclastic/).

## Obtaining

The Pyroclastic Core can be obtained rarely from the [Pyrite Crate](/wiki/crates#pyrite-crate).
