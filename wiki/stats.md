When leveling up [Talents](/wiki/talents)., or when obtaining special items. Players will gain stat boosts. There are 9 available Stats for players to gain boosts in.

## Strength

Strength is a stat that increases attack damage with various weapons. Players gain 0.4 Attack Damage per 1 Strength. At Level 96 in all Talents, players will have a base Strength of 240 or a 96 Attack Damage Trait.

**Sources:**

* [Foraging](/wiki/talents#foraging).
* [Farming](/wiki/talents#farming).
* [Excavation](/wiki/talents#excavation).
* [Archery](/wiki/talents#archery).


## Health

Health is a stat that increases the available HP pool for a player. At Level 96 in all Talents, players will have a base Health of 40 or 2 full health bars.

**Sources:**

* [Agility](/wiki/talents#agility).
* [Fishing](/wiki/talents#fishing).
* [Farming](/wiki/talents#farming).
* [Enchanting](/wiki/talents#enchanting).
* [Foraging](/wiki/talents#foraging).
* [Defense](/wiki/talents#defense).
* [Mining](/wiki/talents#mining).
* [Fighting](/wiki/talents#fighting).
* [Excavation](/wiki/talents#excavation).
* [Alchemy](/wiki/talents#alchemy).

## Regeneration

Regeneration is a stat that increases how fast you recover health and saturation. Players gain 0.02 Saturation- and Hunger Regeneration per 1 Regeneration. At Level 96 in all Talents, players will have a base Regeneration of 240 and 4.8 Hunger- and Saturation Regeneration Trait.

**Sources:**

* [Agility](/wiki/talents#agility).
* [Excavation](/wiki/talents#excavation).
* [Fighting](/wiki/talents#fighting).

## Luck

Luck is a stat that increases the chance of extra drops in gathering Talents. Players gain 0.5 Farming-, Foraging-, Mining-, Excavation-, and Fishing Luck per 1 Luck. At Level 96 in all Talents, players will have a base luck of 192 and
- 296 Excavation Luck
- 296 Farming Luck
- 296 Foraging Luck
- 196 Mining Luck
- 196 Fishing Luck

**Sources:**

* [Mining](/wiki/talents#mining).
* [Fishing](/wiki/talents#fishing).
* [Enchanting](/wiki/talents#enchanting).

## Wisdom

Wisdom is a stat that increases experience gain and decreases anvil costs. Players gain 0.4 Experience Bonus, and 0.1 Anvil Discount, per 1 Wisdom. At Level 96 in all Talents, players will have a base Wisdom of 240 and
- 96 Experience Bonus
- 24 Anvil Discount (44% Effective Anvil Discount)

**Sources:**

* [Agility](/wiki/talents#agility).
* [Alchemy](/wiki/talents#alchemy).
* [Enchanting](/wiki/talents#enchanting).

## Toughness


Toughness is a stat that increases the amount of damage reduced from enemy attacks. Players gain 0.3 Damage Reduction per 1 Toughness. At Level 96 in all Talents, players will have a base Toughness of 240 and
- 72 Damage Reduction (51% Effective Damage Reduction)

**Sources:**

* [Defense](/wiki/talents#defense).
* [Foraging](/wiki/talents#foraging).
* [Mining](/wiki/talents#mining).

## Crit Chance

Crit Chance is a stat that increases the chance of dealing a critical hit. At Level 96 in all Talents, players will have a base Crit Chance of 96%.

**Sources:**

* [Archery](/wiki/talents#archery).

## Crit Damage

Crit Damage is a stat that increases the damage when dealing a critical hit. At Level 96 in all Talents, players will have a base Crit Damage of 96%.

**Sources:**

* [Fighting](/wiki/talents#fighting).

## Speed

Speed is a stat that increases how fast a player moves. Players gain 0.4 Attack Damage per 1 Strength. It does not increase at level up of a Talent. The only source is from the Fleeting perk from [Agility](/wiki/talents#agility).

Base Speed is a speed of 100.

**Sources:**

* Fleeting Perk from [Agility](/wiki/talents#agility).
