Talents is a system for players to perform activities, level them up and receive rewards for their efforts.

## Overview

There are 11 available Talents for players to level up:

* Archery - Earns XP when killing mobs using a projectile.
* Fighting - Earns XP when killing mobs using melee atacks.
* Defense - Earns XP when receiving damage.
* Farming - Earns XP when harvesting crops.
* Foraging - Earns XP when cutting down logs.
* Mining - Earns XP when mining stones and ores.
* Fishing - Earns XP when fishing up items and fish.
* Excavation - Earns XP when digging blocks, such as dirt and sand.
* Agility - Earns XP when receiving fall damage.
* Alchemy - Earns XP when brewing and consuming potions.
* Enchanting - Earns XP when enchanting and repairing items.

## Rewards

Each Talent has a max level of 96, and gives players rewards per level. Some levels have special rewards or perks. 

Every Talent (except Archery) rewards players with an extra half-heart at level 50 and level 96 respectively for a maximum of 10 extra hearts.
