## Player Ranks

Ranks are earned through **playtime, community engagement, and donating** to support the server. Each rank unlocks additional perks and quality-of-life features.

| Rank | How to Obtain | Perks |
|---|---|---|
| **Wanderer** | Default (all new players) | `/sethome` (1 home), `/kit starter` |
| **Settler** | 5 hours playtime | `/sethome` (2 homes), `/nick` |
| **Villager** | 20 hours playtime | `/sethome` (3 homes), coloured chat |
| **Elder** | 50 hours playtime + staff nomination | `/sethome` (5 homes), `/fly` in claims |
| **Patron** | Store donation — Tier 1 | Coloured name, `/fly` everywhere, `/hat` |
| **Arcane** | Store donation — Tier 2 | All Patron perks + particles, priority queue |

<div class="wiki-callout tip">
  <span class="callout-icon"><i class="fas fa-clock"></i></span>
  <div>Check your playtime with <code>/playtime</code>. Rank upgrades are applied automatically — no need to ask staff.</div>
</div>

## Staff Ranks

Staff are volunteers who keep the server running smoothly. They're players first and staff second.

### Helper

The entry-level staff role. Helpers focus on:

- Answering questions from new players
- Moderating chat
- Reporting issues to senior staff

**Tag:** `[Helper]` in green

### Moderator

Full moderation powers. Moderators handle:

- Player reports and ban appeals
- Grief investigations and rollbacks
- Event organisation

**Tag:** `[Mod]` in blue

### Senior Moderator

Experienced moderators with elevated permissions. Responsible for:

- Mentoring Helpers
- Complex punishments and appeals
- Server configuration assistance

**Tag:** `[Sr.Mod]` in dark blue

### Admin

Manages the server's backend, plugins, and major decisions. Admins are appointed by ownership only.

**Tag:** `[Admin]` in gold

### Owner

The founding team of WitchCraftMC. Final authority on all server decisions.

**Tag:** `[Owner]` in red

## How to Apply for Staff

Staff applications open periodically. When applications are open:

1. Check the `#staff-applications` channel on **Discord** for the current form link.
2. Fill out the application honestly — we can tell when answers are exaggerated.
3. Applications are reviewed by Admins and Owners. You'll hear back within **1–2 weeks**.

### What We Look For

- **Genuine helpfulness** — do you already help other players without the tag?
- **Maturity and patience** — can you handle conflict without escalating it?
- **Availability** — we need staff across different timezones.
- **Familiarity with the server** — you should have at least 20 hours of playtime.

<div class="wiki-callout warning">
