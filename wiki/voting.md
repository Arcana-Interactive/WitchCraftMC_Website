Players can vote up to 5 times per real life day, rewarding them with Vote Keys. Much like the other [Crate Keys](/wiki/crates), Vote Keys can be used to open Vote Crates.

Go to [Vote Sites](https://www.witchcraftmc.com/#votes) to see all of our Vote Links.

## Vote Crate Rewards

| Chance | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 20% | Mining Key |
| 20% | Fishing Key |
| 20% | Farming Key |
| 20% | Forest Key |
| 20% | Combat Key |
