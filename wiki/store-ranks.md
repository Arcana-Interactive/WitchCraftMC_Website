The Store Ranks can be purchased from our [Server Store](https://witchcraftmc.tebex.io/) are earned by ranking up through the [Archwizard](/wiki/npc/archwizard) in [Spawn](/wiki/spawn/). Each rank unlocks additional perks and quality-of-life features. You can check your current progress with `/ranks`.

## Permanent Ranks

### Quartz

The **Initiate** rank is the default rank, given to every new player from the start.

**Perks:**
- `/sethome <HomeName>` - 1x saved home
- Access to all standard [commands](/wiki/commands/)

Tag: <span class="rank-badge rank-villager">[Initiate]</span>

---

### Jade

The **Seeker** rank can be obtained by ranking up at the Archwizard for $10,000.

**Perks:**
- `/sethome <HomeName>` - 2x saved home.
- Access to all Initiate perks.

Tag: <span class="rank-badge rank-villager">[Seeker]</span>

---

### Ruby

The **Novice** rank can be obtained by ranking up at the Archwizard for $20,000.

**Perks:**
- `/pv` - 1x 6 row playervault.
- `/ah` - Access to the Auction House.
- Access to all Seeker perks.

Tag: <span class="rank-badge rank-villager">[Novice]</span>
