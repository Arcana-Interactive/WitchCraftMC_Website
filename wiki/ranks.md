## Player Ranks

Ranks are earned through **playtime, community engagement, and store donations**. Each rank unlocks additional perks and quality-of-life features. Check your progress with `/playtime` at any time.

<div class="wiki-callout tip">
  <span class="callout-icon"><i class="fas fa-clock"></i></span>
  <div>Rank upgrades are applied <strong>automatically</strong> — no need to ask staff. Use <code>/playtime</code> to see where you stand.</div>
</div>

## How to Apply for Staff

Staff applications open periodically. When applications are open:

1. Check the `#staff-applications` channel on **Discord** for the current form link.
2. Fill out the application honestly — we can tell when answers are exaggerated.
3. Applications are reviewed by Admins and Owners. You'll hear back within **1–2 weeks**.

### What We Look For

- **Genuine helpfulness** — do you already help other players without the tag?
- **Maturity and patience** — can you handle conflict without escalating it?
- **Availability** — we need staff across different timezones.
- **Familiarity with the server** — you should have at least 20 hours of playtime and know the [rules](/wiki/rules/) well.

<div class="wiki-callout warning">
  <span class="callout-icon"><i class="fas fa-times-circle"></i></span>
  <div>Asking staff members to approve your application will result in an <strong>immediate rejection</strong>. Let the work speak for itself.</div>
</div>
