By using either `/cosmetics` or by entering the **Wardrobe** in [**Spawn**](/wiki/spawn), players can equip Cosmetic Equipment they own. These don't carry any stats, but allows each player to customize how they look.

Any cosmetic equipped through `/cosmetics` or the **Wardrobe** can be equipped even if the player is wearing armour; allowing the player to still get stats from their equipment.

On the rare occassion of a [Season Reset](/wiki/worlds#seasons--world-resets), cosmetics are not reset and remain unlocked permanently.

## Cosmetic Types

| Type | Information |
|:---:|:---|
| **Hats** | Cosmetics appear on player's head. Players with [Quartz Rank](/wiki/store-ranks#quartz) can also use `/hat` to equip any held item onto their head.|
| **Backpacks** | Cosmetics appear on the back of the player.|
| **Hand Items** |Cosmetics appear in the player's offhand slot.|
| **Balloons** | Cosmetics appear floating above the player.|

## How to obtain

Cosmetics can be purchased from [Hatrick](/wiki/npc/hatrick) in Spawn. Some Cosmetics may be unlocked through progression, recipes or limited-time events.
